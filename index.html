Crystals Collector!

Game's Instructions

    1. The computer will generate a target number at random.
    2. You have a choice of four crystals below. By clicking each one will increase your total score randomly.
    3. To win, your total score must equal the target number. If your number exceeds the target number you lose.
    4. The value of each crystal is hidden until selected and reflected in your total score.
    5. Each time the game resets, the crystal's values will change.
